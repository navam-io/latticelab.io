## Turn Research Into Deliverables

Studio is where your research becomes actionable. Transform conversations and insights into polished artifacts that stakeholders can review, approve, and act on.

### Artifact Types

#### Comparison Tables

Side-by-side feature matrices generated from your research conversations:

| Feature | Anthropic Claude | OpenAI GPT-4 | Google Gemini |
|---------|------------------|--------------|---------------|
| Context Window | 200K tokens | 128K tokens | 1M tokens |
| Multimodal | Yes [1] | Yes [2] | Yes [3] |
| Fine-tuning | Limited [4] | Yes [5] | Yes [6] |
| Enterprise SSO | Yes [7] | Yes [8] | Yes [9] |

*All cells include citation links to source documentation*

#### Decision Memos

Executive summaries structured for decision-makers:

```markdown
# AI Vendor Selection: Recommendation Memo

## Executive Summary
Based on analysis of 47 sources across 5 vendors...

## Recommendation
We recommend Anthropic Claude for the following reasons:
1. Superior safety characteristics [1][2]
2. Competitive pricing at scale [3]
3. Strong enterprise features [4]

## Risk Assessment
- Limited fine-tuning options [5]
- Newer vendor with less market presence [6]

## Next Steps
1. Schedule technical proof-of-concept
2. Negotiate enterprise agreement
3. Plan pilot deployment

[Citations with full source references]
```

#### Research Reports

Comprehensive analysis documents with:

- Problem statement and methodology
- Detailed findings with citations
- Comparative analysis
- Recommendations and conclusions
- Full bibliography of sources

### Citation Preservation

Every artifact maintains full citation integrity:

- **Inline Citations** — [1], [2], [3] throughout the document
- **Bibliography** — Complete source list at the end
- **Source Links** — Click-through to original documents
- **Timestamp** — When each source was accessed

### Version History

Never lose your work:

- Automatic saves as you edit
- Named versions for milestones
- Compare different versions
- Restore previous versions
